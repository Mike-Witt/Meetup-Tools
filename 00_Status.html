General Scheme

  • The reason for the separate "member dictionary" and "location dictionary" is so that we can (in the member dict) maintain the original form of the information obtained from meetup, and try different techniques to parse it, without having to hit the meetup sites again.

  • Right now make-map does everything, based on the flags given it:
    ./make-map -D my-group-name basically does everything
    ./make-map my-group-name will build everything if the member dict exists
    ./make-map -dm my-group-name shows the member dict
    ./make-map -dl my-group-name shows the location dict
